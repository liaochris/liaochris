Research

Outside of academics, I have worked on both economics and computer science research. My GitHub profile is linked here and all the associated code for my research projects, is available there. Below is a listing of the projects I have worked on in the past and am working on in the present.

Occupation Productivity - in progress

Project with UChicago Financial Economics graduate student James Traina on the relationship between the compensation and occupational productivity of a worker.
GitHub repo: https://github.com/liaochris/occupation-productivity

FCC Broadband Deployment - in progress

Work for Chicago Booth graduate student Uyen Tran on broadband deployment, uptake and market concentration across the United States.
GitHub repo: https://github.com/liaochris/Uyen_FCC_work

Stretch Robot @ UChicago HRI Lab - completed

RA for UChicago CS Professor Sarah Sebo on creating demos for Hello Robot's flagship product the Stretch Robot that involved navigating and picking up objects, and using audio and recording features.

Just War Theory - completed

Independent research paper on the intersection of morality and territorial disputes using the Senkaku Islands as a case study. My paper was published in the June 2020 edition of The Concord Review.
